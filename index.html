How To Play Canuckle – A Daily Canadian Word Game?

Canuckle is played similarly to the other Wordle game. The user must correctly guess five-letter terms linked to Canada in order to win the game. Three highlighting colours will be present when you begin to guess; these are described below, along with their functions.

  • Red: The highlighted red color means the letter is in the correct position and should not be changed.
  • Yellow: The highlighted yellow color indicates the letter is in the word but in the wrong position. You should try placing it somewhere else in your next guess.
  • Grey: The highlighted grey color means the letter is not in the word at all, so you should avoid using it in your next attempts.
